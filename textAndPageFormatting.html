Text Formatting

HTML Formatting is a process that allows us to format text to increase its visual appeal. Various HTML tags can change how text appears on a web page and make the text attractive.

Bold Text

Anything that appears within < b >...< /b > elements, is displayed in bold typeface.

Italic Text

Anything that appears within < i >...< /i > elements, is displayed in italicized typeface.

Underlined Text

Anything that appears within < u >...< /u > elements, is displayed in underlined typeface.

Subscript Text

Anything that appears within < sub >...< /sub > elements, is displayed in Subscript typeface.

Smaller Text

Anything that appears within < small >...< /small > elements, is displayed in Smaller typeface.

Superscript Text

Anything that appears within < sup >...< /sup > elements, is displayed in Superscript typeface.

Marked Text

Anything that appears within < mark >...< /mark > elements, is displayed in Mark typeface.

Deleted Text

Anything that appears within < del >...< /del > elements, is displayed in Deleted typeface.
